Glue Butterfly Painting

By: Katarina

Creator's Note

Materials

  • Canvas
  • Hot glue stick
  • Paint
  • Paint brushes

Steps

  1. Paint the background with the color of your choice.
  2. Trace the Butterfly pattern with hot glue stick.
  3. Wait for it to cool down.
  4. Paint the inside of the Butterfly.
  5. Paint the hot glue stick.

Your Notes

Materials

Description

This project involves creating a vibrant butterfly art piece using hot glue and paint. Start by painting the background with a color of your choice to set the stage for your artwork. Next, trace the butterfly pattern on the background using a hot glue stick, ensuring the lines are well-defined. Allow the hot glue to cool and harden completely before moving to the next step. Once the glue is set, paint the inside of the butterfly, carefully staying within the glued outlines, and finish by painting over the hot glue lines to add dimension and detail to your butterfly.
